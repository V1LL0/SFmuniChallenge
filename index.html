SF Muni Challenge

Instructions

Click the buttons to show the corresponding route!

Click the Show Stops button to show or hide the bus stops.

You can also click on the elements of the map:

  • Buses: click a bus to show a tooltip with info about the vehicle
  • Bus stops: click a stop to have predictions on arrivals
  • Map: click a point in the map or in the water to hide an showed tooltip
